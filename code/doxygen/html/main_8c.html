void Initialize_Heater ( void  )

Heater initialization routine.

Initializes heater to a starting voltage of less than 2V during the condensation phase. Once past condensation phase heater voltage is ramped up at a rate of 0.4V/s until reaching a maximum of 13V. This is highlighted in the LSU 4.9 manual.
